With Gratitude and Joy – Thank You!

On behalf of the entire team at Internationale Deutsche Schule Sarajevo, we would like to express our heartfelt gratitude for the incredible honor of receiving the 2025 SDG Business Pioneers Award in the category "Small Company – People."

It is a privilege to be recognized alongside other remarkable companies across Bosnia and Herzegovina. This award, presented by UNDP, UNICEF, and the Government of Sweden, holds deep meaning for our small but passionate community, where every child, educator, and parent matters.

We are humbled and inspired to continue fostering a safe, inclusive, and values-driven environment for children while staying connected to our broader community.

Thank you for supporting and celebrating education with heart.
